SporeX

A top down shooter online multiplayer game.

Based on a Box2D physics engine featuring collision detection, movable objects and real world-like armor/penetration system where reduced armor is equal to the armor multiplied by the cosine of the bullet angle to armor normal.

Includes feature-rich interface, 15 different tanks (each with its own unique hull & turret armor scheme), 4 playable maps & much more.

Features fully working online multiplayer with lobbies, matchmaking & leaderboard. Networking is based on KryoNet and features full server-side physic calculations, with client-side only rendering received values.

The JRE is packed within the launcher, therefore no Java installation is needed in order to play.
